Modal Reader

by Bailey Liang

Double-click text on a webpage to open it in a modal reader designed to improve readability.
Enable extension

Control whether or not this extension is active.
Hide reader on-scroll.

Hide the modal reader whenever you scroll on the main webpage.
Insert line break after sentence.

Insert a line break after each sentence inside the modal reader. (Experimental)
Double line breaks.

Line breaks are applied twice inside the modal reader.
Detect system dark mode.

Changes the modal reader's theme based on your browser's theme settings.
Highlight readable elements on-hover.

Webpage elements that can be read inside the modal reader are highlighted whenever your mouse cursor hovers over them. (Experimental)
Copy modal reader content to clipboard.

When you double-click to open the modal reader, the text inside the modal reader also gets copied to your clipboard automatically.
